Cooking or Cooked

'Cooking or Cooked' is a cookbook that is designed by The Lazy Chefs. It contains 50 recipes that quick and easy to make. The cookbook is designed to help university students become more health. It includes recipes from different cultures as well.

Why Must I Be Healthy?

Maintaing a healthy physical wellbeing can support your emotional wellbeing too. By eating the correct food and exercising, you will stay energetic, be more relaxed and easily fight off illness. Teenagers should sustain good eating habits and exercise regularly which will promote a healthy lifestyle and good living.
